The Legacy Project

A Little Bit About

At The Legacy Project, we believe in the transformative power of every individual's name. We recognize that within each person lies the potential to rewrite their story and leave a legacy of resilience and transformation. As individuals who have stood on the front lines of addiction and witnessed the struggles firsthand, we understand the critical need for comprehensive support systems to bridge the gap between inpatient treatment programs and sustainable sober living transitions.

More About Us
Too often, individuals completing their treatment programs find themselves faced with the daunting reality of limited options for continued support. The absence of available sober living transitions can lead to devastating consequences, including homelessness and a regression into destructive habits. This glaring gap in care underscores the urgent necessity for initiatives like The Legacy Project.

Our name, 'The Legacy Project,’ is more than just a title; it embodies our unwavering commitment to empowering individuals to reclaim their lives and build a future filled with purpose and possibility. We recognize that addiction is not just an individual struggle but a generational burden, passed down through legacies of pain and unaddressed trauma. By offering a supportive environment where individuals can heal and thrive, we aim to break the cycle of addiction and create a new legacy of wellness and resilience.

Central to our mission is the belief in the inherent potential within each person to overcome adversity and achieve greatness. Through personalized support and holistic care, we guide residents on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment, helping them unlock the untapped resources within themselves to navigate the challenges of recovery and beyond.

The Legacy Project is more than just a transitional housing facility; it is a beacon of hope and a testament to the transformative power of community. We understand that true recovery extends beyond individual efforts and requires the collective support of a caring and compassionate network. As such, we invite the community and potential supporters to join us in our mission to extend the reach of our services and create a culture of recovery that thrives on hope, empowerment, and renewed purpose.

Together, we can turn the tide against addiction and build a future where every individual has the opportunity to rewrite their story, reclaim their identity, and leave behind a legacy of resilience and transformation. Join us in harnessing the power of names and building a brighter tomorrow for generations to come.


About Me

Marcos Gomez

CEO and Founder of The Legacy Project

Welcome to The Legacy Project! We are Marcos and Elizabeth Gomez. We’ve walked the challenging path of addiction together, Marcos as an addict and Elizabeth trying to provide support. We know the pain of feeling hopeless. Our first step was for Marcos to enter rehabilitation. Stepping into rehabilitation gave him insight into the components necessary for a fully transformative process. He witnessed firsthand the profound need for sustained support beyond the initial stages of recovery. While many of us were just beginning our restoration process, we were faced with the reality of an absence of stable housing for continued recovery utside the walls of rehabilitation. This often led to heartbreaking relapses. Determined to break this cycle, he made a personal pledge to establish a facility that would provide ongoing support for those courageously committed to a life of sobriety.
